Straight answers

Investor FAQ

Is this gambling?

No. It's a purely skill-based competition — no randomness, no wagering, no odds-based payouts — so it sits outside gambling regulation. Every prize is fully insured and pre-funded.

What if someone actually wins R1,000,000?

The insurer pays. Every prize is 100% underwritten by Santam and verified by dual cameras plus an AI shot tracer. A big winner is the best marketing day of the year.

How do the course partnerships work?

Courses earn 10% of every entry with zero upfront cost, on exclusive 2-year auto-renewing agreements with restraint of trade; we cover installation, insurance, tech and operations. We install on the signature par-3 and expand where demand justifies it — Metropolitan and Boschenmeer already run two holes each.

Who else does this? What's the moat?

An always-on, permanently installed, verified hole-in-one platform is a world first; the only meaningful local competitor was acquired and rebranded into Get Lucky. The moat: exclusive locked-in course contracts, owned verification tech and a first-party platform — compounded by Ernie's name. Promo days that reframe the risk doubled entries (~25% of entrants hit the green).

How do the two entry models fit together?

Installed courses (dual cameras, physical entry board) anchor the brand and keep 78% of every entry. The phone model scales to any par-3 on earth with no installation cost. Installs create legitimacy; phone entries create reach.

What exactly am I buying in this round?

10% of Get Lucky Golf Club (Pty) Ltd for R4m at a R40m post-money valuation, alongside the founders and founding investor Ernie Els. Full terms, the cap table and the valuation model are in the dataroom.
